About Us

Welcome to LTS !

LTS was founded in 2022. Our shop was built from a love of tacos 🌮. LTS is a culinary haven born out of a genuine love for tacos, crafted with devotion and served with a side of community spirit. Nestled in the heart of our charming town, we take pride in adding a unique and delightful touch to our community's culinary landscape.

Taco Trivia

When did tacos first appear in the United States?

Jeffrey M. Pulcher, taco historian and professor of history at the University of Minnesota, says the earliest mention of tacos in the United States are in the newspaper from 1905 (Source = Smithsomian Magazine).
